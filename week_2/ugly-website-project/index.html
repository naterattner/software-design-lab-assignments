Meet the Mets: New York's Lovable? Losers

In 2023, the Mets entered the season with the largest payroll in baseball and expectations to compete for a championship. They will finish the season with a losing record and without many of their star players, who they traded away midseason.

This is the just the latest chapter in a long history of embarassing Mets sagas.

Here are some of the most notorious Mets seasons in the team's 61-year history:

  • 1962: In their first-ever season, the Mets go 40-120, posting one of the worst records ever
  • 1993: Mets lose 100 games for the fierst time since 1967
  • 2006: Mets' best hitter watches strike 3 go by to lose NL Championship series
  • 2007: With 17 games left in the season, the Mets blew a seven-game NL east lead
  • 2008: Same thing as the year before but a 3.5 game lead
  • 2023: Pay players a lot, win a little
